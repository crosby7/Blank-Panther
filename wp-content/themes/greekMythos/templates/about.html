About the Philosophers

Learn more about the creators behind the scenes below! How did these great Philosophers develop this resourceful site you may ask, dear mortal? It all began at a cafe during the night time, where they all scheduled a dinner with each other. Each shared their love for Ancient Greece, and came up with the idea of creating the best way to share such information with the world: a website! The rest is history- or should we say- Ancient History!

Zeus Hendrix - Creator of Greek Mythos

Lucillian Randalar - Co-Creator

Leonard Cobalt - Researcher and Designer

Zeus Hendrix has always had a passion for Greek Mythology, and wanted to make the knowledge of Ancient Greece accessible to the world. Every currently teaches at Greecian University as the Professor of Ancient History. His name matches his favorite greek god, and his powerful passion matches that name well.

Lucillian Randalar is the Co-Creator of GreekMythos. She developed the idea of this site with her best friend and creator Zeus Hendrix. The pair met in college and have found that they shared interest in Ancient Greece. Today, she works at the Ancients Musuem, studying new artifacts found each day and conducting tours.

Leonard Cobalt is a graphic designer and researcher that currently works at the Ancients Museum. Leonard first met Lucillian at the museum, and found her and Zeus's idea exciting. He currently conducts research in order to provide monthly fascinating articles about artifacts that were found in other countries. Lots of posters and graphics found at the Greecian University and Ancients Museum were created courtesy of Leonard Cobalt.
