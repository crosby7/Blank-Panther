Zeus: Ruler of the Gods-Books Many Vacations to Earth

Zeus is considered the ruler of the Greek gods, yet in many tales he is presented as a more social character. Most people in royalty do not interact with their subjects personally, at least that is what we hear in the tales of yore. Yet Zeus was considered an interesting case. By today's standards, he would be considered unfaithful to his wife, for many of his stories involved an object of romance. Hera, who was his wife, would often be depicted as a vengeful woman on the subject, and would many times subject Zeus's women to terrors beyond their imagination. That would be difficult to discuss at the dinner table on Thanksgiving would it not?
